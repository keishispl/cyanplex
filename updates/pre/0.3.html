We also added additional commands for different uses.


/guide - Guide

Rarity (/rarity)
Dimensions (/dimensions)

Mana & Speed (/mana-speed)

The Guide NPC is found at spawn.



/stats [player] - Get stats of yourself, or someone else

You can also find this in the button at the bottom-right of guide


/settings - Enable and disable features

You can also find this in the button at the bottom of guide


Current list of settings:

Sprint Dash - Some people can maneuver better without it

New Movement Options

Double Jump (Pressing Jump Twice)

It will launch you into the air

Dash (Pressing Sneak)

It will push you forward

Sprint Dash (By Sprinting)

It will push you a little bit forward every 2 seconds, can be disabled in /settings
